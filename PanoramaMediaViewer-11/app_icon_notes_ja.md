# App Icon Notes

## Design

- Simple dark background
- Central `360` text
- Subtle sphere/grid lines indicating panoramic viewing
- Minimal file-list lines near the bottom
- No third-party logo, trademark, or brand name
- No transparency; iOS applies the rounded mask automatically

## Files

The icon images are stored in:

```text
PanoramaMediaViewer/PanoramaMediaViewer/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/
```

The 1024px preview is also saved as:

```text
app_icon_preview_1024.png
```
